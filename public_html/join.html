Application

Download the application for Delta Delta Sigma here.

Application for Delta Delta Sigma membership is non-competitive. Annual dues are $15.00, accepted as cash or check (payable to Delta Delta Sigma), and may be submitted with the application at any Delta Delta Sigma event. Alternatively, you may contact dental@uw.edu to arrange a meeting with one of the officers.

Benefits

  • We are the largest pre-dental club on campus, allowing you opportunity to meet fellow pre-dentals
  • Announcements about such opportunities below are made available only to members
  • Networking with the dental community and other pre-dental students
  • Guidnce as a pre-dental student throughout your time as an undergrad
  • Dental student and professional informational panels
  • Frequent and diverse volunteer opportunities
  • Discounts on any Dental Admission Test (DAT) courses from Kaplan Test Prep
  • Job opportunities such as assisting, research, etc.
  • Opportunity to run for following year's officer positions
  • Free food and drink at meetings!
