Welcome to Delta Delta Sigma, The University of Washington's Pre-Dental Society.

Please explore the site to learn about our upcoming pre-dental activities, links to valuable resources, and information about what you can do to attain your goal of becoming a dental professional. Join Delta Delta Sigma now to learn how to apply to dental school, become involved in leadership and volunteer opportunities, meet dentists and dental students, and explore the dental field. We only require an annual fee of $15 to help us bring these pre-dental opportunities to the UW community! Be sure to check out our Facebook Page! Find pictures from our club meetings and volunteer events there.
