Welcome to Delta Delta Sigma. Please explore our site to find out about upcoming pre-dental activities, links to invaluable resources, and information about what you can do to attain your goal of becoming a dental professional. We hope you're having a great summer. Stay tuned to hear about all of the great upcoming events in the fall.

Join Delta Delta sigma now to become more involved and become better prepared for dental school. To Become involved with leadership, volunteering, getting to know dentists and and how to prepare for dental school, only requires an annual fee of $10! Become a member today!Also check out our Facebook site UW DDS Facebook Link.

We are proud to announce Delta Delta Sigma's involvement with ECOH (Early Childhood Oral Health), Children's Hospital and UWSoD's joint effort in combating childhood dental disease. Please check out our ECOH link for more information. We have an upcoming meeting discussing our involvement with ECOH- stay tuned for details.
