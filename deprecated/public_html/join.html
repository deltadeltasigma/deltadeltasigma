Delta Delta Sigma

University of Washington's Pre-Dental Society

Join the Club!

There are several reasons to become a D.D.S. member:

  • Receive announcements given only to members, such as dental student panels and volunteer opportunites
  • 10% off on any Kaplan (test prep) classroom course
  • Ability to run for next year's officer positions
  • D.D.S. is the biggest pre-dental club on UW campus, allowing you plenty of opportunities to meet fellow pre-dental students and learn more about what being a dentist entails
  • Free food provided at meetings!

Services partially restricted to members:

Students who wish to see what the club is like before joining are encouraged to contact the club officers via dental@uw.edu. Upon request, non-members are added to our e-mail list to be notified about *select* events open to all guests. For example, only members will have access to volunteer events and Q/A panels with admissions officers, practicing dentists, and dental students. Our officers strive to advise and dispense information to anyone with an interest in the dental field, as well as direct all pre-dental students to valuable experiences in dental outreach and volunteer events. These opportunities are only made possible through the monetary support that members of D.D.S. provide.

How to join:

  1. Fill out a membership application. Applications can be downloaded by clicking HERE (Word File) or picked up at any D.D.S. event. To request that an application be e-mailed to you, contact us at dental@uw.edu. Applications can be turned in at any Delta Delta Sigma event.
  2. Yearly dues for D.D.S are $15.00. They can be paid by cash or check (payable to Delta Delta Sigma) at any D.D.S. event or by contacting the club email to arrange a meeting with one of the officers.
