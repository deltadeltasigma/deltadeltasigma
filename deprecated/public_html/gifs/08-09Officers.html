Webmaster: Katie Rowley

Major(s): Biology (Cellular, Molecular, and Developmental)
Intended Graduation Date: June 2009
Why Dentistry? Because dentistry plays an important part in people's physical and emotional health and I would love the opportunity to provide such a valuable service in my community. Also, dentistry is very detail-oriented, as well as creative, which is perfect for me!
Who most inspires you? I'm inspired by people that are fascinated by the world around them and try to make it a more interesting place. My husband is top on that list.
What interests do you have, other than dentistry? Traveling, scuba diving, skiing, reading, glass beadmaking, cheesemaking, knitting, quilting, pottery...
What's on your MP3 player? I've got a really big mix on my ipod, but I usually use it to listen to classical music when I'm studying. I listen to the radio a lot- mostly KEXP.
What is your favorite book? "The Moor's Last Sigh" by Salman Rushdie, maybe. Well, practically anything by Salman Rushdie or Gabriel Garcia Marquez.
Where do you see yourself in 10 years? I see myself as the local dentist in a small town, providing dental services in underserved overseas communities when possible, happily spending time with my husband and enjoying life.
