Office Hours


The Pre-dental Society's office is located in the HUB sub-basement, room SB-41. During office hours, you can get information about dental schools and admission processes, find volunteer positions, or just hang out and chat with officers and members. Due to busy class schedules, office hours may end 10 minutes earlier than noted on the schedule.
