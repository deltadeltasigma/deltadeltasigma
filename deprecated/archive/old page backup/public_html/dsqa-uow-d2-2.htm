University of Washington - D2, Class of 2004    

1.  What suggestions do you have to pre-dental students getting ready for dental school?

Try to take more science classes than just those required.    Enjoy yourselves doing it! 

2.  Do you feel you have made the right decision on the school you are attending? 

Yes.  Great school with a lot to offer. 

3.  What are some strengths and weaknesses of the school? 

 Strengths:  The best dental education in the country and certainly one of the best in the world     Weaknesses:  Self taught 

 4.  How is the student/teacher ratio?  What is your impression of the faculty? 

 Good.  Never had a problem with not having questions answered. 

 5.  Do you get enough clinical experience? 

 Hard to say.  I am still a second year.  From what I hear of the upper classmen, they have plenty.  We could use more as second years though. 

6.  How competitive is the class?  Are classes with medical students?

Very competitive, but every class is different.  Two classes with medical students: Gross Anatomy and Head and Neck Anatomy.  Rumor has it that its going to change soon and the dental students will be doing it alone.  Could be good or bad. 

7.  How's city life and the weather? 

Weather: Late October to Mid-May = Boo . . . Mid-May to Late Octeober = no place more beautiful - sunny and green 

8.  If you wish to add additional comments. . . 

 Hang in there!
