Our Profile

    The Delta Delta Sigma Pre-Dental club is a recognized student organization at the University of Washington. Members of the Delta Delta Sigma are introduced to other pre-dental students and current UW dental students to create a community of people with a common goal. Through this community network, members can gain advice on "Do's and Don'ts" for pre-dental preparation while being informed about current admissions for the University of Washington School of Dentistry and many other schools across the nation.                                 

Contact Information
