How to Join     

Sign Me Up!                       

Interested in joining Delta Delta Sigma? Don't worry, there are several ways to join

  • Send an e-mail containing your name, address, phone, e-mail, and  year/major to either dental@u.washington.edu or to the secretary Jessica Toyama jpmt@u.washington.edu

  • Or, attend one of our club meetings. We work at scheduling many informative meetings that are convenient for everyone. Check out the events page to for the next meeting. 

  • Sign up right NOW! Download an application, fill it out, and drop it off at our office HUB SB41 or mail to:

                    SAO Box 149, UW Box 352238
                    Seattle, WA 98195-2238
