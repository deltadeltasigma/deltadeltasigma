ECOH (Early Childhood Oral Health)


In order to better protect young children from oral disease, Children's Hospital and the University of Washington School of Dentistry are joining together to develop a new pediatric facility, as part of the Early Child Oral Health (ECOH) program. This new facility, located in Magnuson Park, is expected to serve more than 30,000 patients a year! As you can imagine, they will need a great deal of volunteers. This is where Delta Delta Sigma comes in. As requested by the ECOH program manager, in order to be a student volunteer at the facility, you must be a Delta Delta Sigma member. There will be a number of ways to participate and we will discuss the details of how to get involved at meeting to be announced. This is a don't miss meeting if you are interested in volunteering with ECOH.
