Join the Club


There are several reasons to become a D.D.S. member:

  • Participation in ECOH requires DDS membership!!!
  • Receive announcements given only to members, such as select events and volunteer opportunites
  • $100 rebate on any Kaplan (test prep) classroom course
  • Ability to run for next year's officer positions
  • Participate in the Annual Kaplan course online auction-- open only to D.D.S.
  • Participate in the annual June drawing for a free Kaplan course

Services not restricted to members:

Students who wish to be involved with the club as non-members (or to see what the club is like before joining) are encouraged to come to the D.D.S. office during office hours, to events organized by D.D.S., and to be added to our e-mail list. Our officers and members strive to advise and dispense information to anyone with an interest in the dental field, as well as direct all pre-dental students to valuable opportunities to gain experience in dental and other healthcare settings.

How to join:

  1. Fill out a membership application. Applications can be downloaded (Word File), or picked up at all D.D.S. events and during office hours. To request that an application be e-mailed to you, contact us at dental@u.washington.edu. Applications can be turned in at any Delta Delta Sigma event or mailed to the address below.
  2. Yearly dues for D.D.S are $15.00. They can be paid by cash or check (payable to Delta Delta Sigma) at any D.D.S. event. Also, you can pay by credit card using the WePay button below.
