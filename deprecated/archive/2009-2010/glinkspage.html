Dental Volunteering
Are you interested in volunteering in public health dentistry? The Union Gospel Mission operates a busy clinic in downtown Seattle. They are seeking qualified pre-dents to volunteer as dental assistants. Please visit the mission's webpage at ugm.org to fill out a volunteer application. After you have submitted the application and attended an orientation program, you can be eligible to participate. It is recommended that you be up-to-date with your immunizations and vaccinations. If you are a qualifed UW SoD volunteer, you can bring this documentation with you to the UGM orientation. (WSP background check, Hall Health, etc) These forms are available on this site.

If you are interested in Volunteering at the UW School of Dentistry, there are many positions available at the SOD Pre-Doctoral Clinic. For more information you can contact Jay Priebe at his email priebej@u.washington.edu or his office phone: (206)685-7601.
