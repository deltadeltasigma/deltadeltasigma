DDS Scholarship Contest


The officers of DDS are excited to annunce a scholarship contest for our members. There will be two scholarships, each in the amount of $250.00 that will go towards anything that is for dental school applications. These scholarships are intended to assist two students to better afford the dental application process, such as costs for DATs or application fees, because we believe everyone deserves an equal opportuity in preparing and applying to dental school. Last year, no one applied and the money went unused, so don't let this opportunity go by!


Below are the guidelines on how to apply for the scholarship:

1. You must be a DDS member.

2. Submit a one to two page, double-spaced essay on the following: Something (person/ place/ event) that has inspired you recently to pursue a career in dentistry. For example, something you observed in your volunteer work recently, or someone you heard speak at an event, etc. When submitting your essay, please include your last name, first name, year in college, major, and e-mail address.

3. Please send your essays via email at dental@u.washington.edu or to our mailbox located in Student Activities Office on the 2nd floor of the HUB: Box 149.

4. The deadline is Tuesday May 6, 2007 at 11:00 pm. Recipients will be announced within two weeks of the deadline. Good luck!
